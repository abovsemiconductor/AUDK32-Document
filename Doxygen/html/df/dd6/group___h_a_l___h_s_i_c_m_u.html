HSICMU
HAL (Hardware Abstraction Layer)

Detailed Description

High-Speed Internal OSC Clock Management Unit.

  • The High-Speed Internal OSC Clock Management Unit (HSICMU) is designed for the auto-trimming of the Internal OSC clock.
